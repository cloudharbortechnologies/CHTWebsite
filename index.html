DevOps as a Service (DaaS)

OIt is a model where organizations can outsource or subscribe to a set of DevOps capabilities rather than building and maintaining them in-house. This approach allows companies to focus on their core business while leveraging external expertise and tools to streamline their software development and delivery processes.

Site Reliability Engineering

SRE is a discipline that incorporates aspects of software engineering and applies them to infrastructure and operations problems. SREs are responsible for creating scalable and highly reliable software systems.

Monitoring

A Cloud Monitoring Team is responsible for overseeing and managing the monitoring, observability, and performance of an organization's cloud infrastructure and services. This team plays a critical role in ensuring the reliability, security, and optimal performance of applications and systems deployed in the cloud.

Setup Cloud Infrastructure

cloud infrastructure refers to the collection of hardware and software resources provided by cloud service providers over the internet. These resources include computing power, storage, networking, databases, and other services that enable users to build, deploy, and manage applications without the need for physical hardware.


Monolithic Architecture to Microservices

Migrating from a monolithic architecture to microservices involves breaking down a large, tightly integrated application into smaller, loosely coupled services. This transition allows for greater flexibility, scalability, and agility in development and deployment.


CSP Migration

Migrating from one cloud service provider (CSP) to another involves moving your applications, data, and infrastructure from the current provider's environment to a new one.
